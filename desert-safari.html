Going on a Dubai Desert Safari 

Picture this: a mesmerizing sunset, replete with stunning shades of yellows and reds and a hint of pink streaking through. The sun’s rays fall softly on an endless expanse of golden sands and you’re left in awe at the natural beauty of the desert in front of you. Contrary to popular opinion that a desert pales in front of the mountains or the sea, visiting one is a unique experience in itself. 

Make memories of a lifetime by indulging in a plethora of activities that are both thrilling and enchanting. Crash into the dunes in a Dune Buggy or slide down the sand on a Sandboard, or go on a relaxing Camel Ride; Dubai Desert Safaris have something for everyone. 

Why Go on a Dubai Desert Safari?

  • Sandy Landscape: Explore the beautiful sandy landscape as you learn about the desert wildlife. 
  • Rich Arabian Culture: Immerse yourself in the rich Arabian culture and heritage as you enjoy your entertainment-filled nights. 
  • Delectable Cuisines: Indulge in delectable Arabian delicacies like Gahwa, Gaymat, Rogag, and more along with an unlimited barbecue. 
  • Desert Camps: Sleep under the stars at a desert camp and wake up to a beautiful sunrise over the sand dunes. 
  • Breathtaking Views: Witness the sunlight bounce off the sands for amazing photographs that rival desktop screensavers.

All Your Dubai Desert Safari Ticket Options Explained

As the Dubai Desert is quite a distance from the city limits, all the desert safaris will include professional guides and transfers from your hotel or specific pickup points. Here are some of the most popular Dubai Desert Safari experiences that you should definitely consider on your vacation.

Morning Safaris

These safaris start quite early in the morning to allow you to witness the rays of the rising sun glisten off the sand. Depending on your ticket type, you can indulge in adventure activities, enjoy a lovely picnic breakfast in the desert or even head out to explore the desert wildlife. 

Evening Safaris

While the daylight is best to indulge in adventure activities, the nighttime is always brimming with live entertainment shows in the Dubai Desert. Picking an evening desert safari in Dubai will allow you to experience the rich Arabian culture while also indulging in delectable desert cuisines. 

Adventure Safaris

One of the biggest highlights of Dubai Desert Safaris is the sheer number of activities the adventurer in you can explore. Go over the sand dunes by yourself in a quad bike, ride a range rover with your family, or take a camel ride with your children, the desert has something for everyone. 

Heritage Safaris

Heritage Desert Safaris are curated for the culture vulture in you. Spend a few hours living like an Emirati as you indulge yourself in the rich Bedouin culture. Wear traditional garments, spend the night in an authentic Bedouin tent, and listen to some memorable local stories on these safaris. 

Nature Safaris

If you’re the kind of explorer who loves to discover the wildlife of your travel destinations, then Nature Desert Safaris is your perfect pick. With professional guides, you get to interact with the desert wildlife and also learn about some of the conservation techniques to protect their sandy homes. 

Overnight Safaris

You can even go camping in the Dubai Desert with Overnight Desert Safari packages. Nights in the desert are filled with live entertainment, delicious barbeque, traditional Arabic cuisines and sleeping under the stars. You can also listen to interesting folktales and take a few puffs from the shisha. 

Luxury Safaris

If you’re someone who prefers to be pampered, then pick a Luxury Desert Safari for a lavish experience. These safaris include private transfers in Range Rovers, a traditional experience with Arabic headscarves, and even a delectable 6-course meal in one of the best resorts in the desert. 

Combo Tickets

Combo Dubai Desert Safari tickets allow you to make the most of your visit to Dubai. These tickets allow you to explore some of the biggest attractions of Dubai along with exploring the desert. And what’s more is that you can even get amazing deals and discounts online on your combo tickets!

Dubai Desert Safari Activities

Dune Bashing

A spine-tingling adventure awaits you when you opt for dune-bashing in Dubai! Step into a 4x4 vehicle, usually a Land Cruiser or Fortuner, and set off into the desert. Hold on tight as your driver races along the sand dunes at varying speeds, while you slide, bounce and skid off your seat giving you a complete adrenaline rush. Your driver is a trained professional who will maneuver the 4x4 vehicle expertly up and down the golden sand dunes.

Dune Buggy

If you find Dune Bashing thrilling, a Dune Buggy would certainly be electrifying. With this, you get the chance to ride a powerful beast that doesn't just have impressive horsepower but also advanced safety features. An expert guide will give you all the tips to keep yourself safe while you head on a dirt-tromping adventure. This sand vehicle is open to everyone 18 and above and doesn’t even require you to have a driver’s license!

Quad Biking

Much like a Dune Buggy, a Quad Bike is also for a solo adventurer to tear up the dunes. You can ride a 150cc Quad Bike with low-pressure tires that allow you to easily drive through the slippery sands. While a Dune Buggy would be suitable for those seeking extreme sports, Quad Biking is more suitable for everyone else and requires minimal assistance. So go ahead and challenge your buddies to a thrilling quad bike race!

Sandboarding

Sandboarding in Dubai is not for the fainthearted. Strap on your gear and step onto the golden dunes of Dubai’s deserts. For sandboarding, a specially-designed board will be fastened to your feet with which you can smoothly slide and glide across the slopes of the Arabian desert. This activity is completely safe and can be experienced throughout the year. The cherry on top? It is a solo sport and requires no tandem assistance. 

Camel Rides

Since ancient Arabia, camels have been known as the Ship of the Desert. They were the primary source of transport from one place to another for local tribes, as well as carriers of goods. You can feel like you’ve stepped back in time by opting for a lovely camel ride in Dubai. A guide will inform you about the various steps to take before and during a camel ride so as to set the animal at ease. Hop on and enjoy the view!

Entertainment

The nighttime in the desert is just as lively as the daylight with many live shows to keep you entertained. You can enjoy a delectable barbecue meal while you watch entertainers make stunning performances in Tanoura, Nashaat and fire dancing. You can also get a traditional Shisha pot prepared for you to enjoy in various flavors, and if you have the taste for it, then you can also immerse yourself in beautiful Henna paintings. 

Desert Wildlife

The desert is home to a wide variety of plants and animals and a lot of times, it’s difficult to spot them. With an expert guide, you can learn a lot about the life that thrives in the desert. You can visit a camel farm, watch falconry shows, and even interact with other animals in the desert. Enjoy camel feeding while you also learn about the different conservation techniques used to protect desert wildlife. 

Sunrise & Sunsets

You must have seen a lot of wallpapers and screensavers of the beautiful panoramic views of sand dunes. Now imagine yourself being in one of these pictures! With the rays of the sun glistening beautifully off the golden sands, watching the sunrise or sunset in the Dubai Desert should definitely be on your vacation bucket list. Watch the sky change colors as you drive through the sands or strike a pose for some professional photography as well!

The location of your Dubai Desert Safari is quite a distance from the city limits. However, with round-trip transfers, you don’t have to worry about getting to the location by yourself. You can simply give the name or location of your hotel and an air-conditioned vehicle will pick you up and also drop you off after your safari.

The most popular Dubai desert safari locations include Al Aweer Desert, Hatta Mountains and the Dubai Desert Convention Reserve amongst others. Each location provides a different experience; at some, you might get to experience the traditional Bedouin culture, while at others you might get to witness interesting flora and fauna common to desert landscapes. 

Here are a few things you should keep in mind when going on a Dubai Desert Safari. 

  • Book your tickets online and beforehand to properly plan your experience. 
  • Never leave without sunscreen. Even if you spend most of your time in the AC vehicle, you can still get nasty sunburns. 
  • Stay hydrated. You can always ask your guide for some water. 
  • Carry sunglasses or shades to protect your eyes. 
  • Travel light as you will move around quite a bit. 
  • Always follow the instructions of your guide or instructor. Your safety comes first.

It is important to dress appropriately when going on a Dubai Desert Safari, especially if you want to indulge in adventure activities. 

  • Wear loose, comfortable, cotton or linen clothing. Try to avoid synthetics. 
  • Carry a sweater or jacket to keep yourself warm when the temperature drops drastically in the evenings. 
  • Avoid wearing clothes that reveal too much skin as this isn’t appreciated by the locals. When confused, remember that you have to keep your shoulders and knees covered. 
  • If you want to wear shorts, make sure they’re at least mid-thigh length.
  • Avoid short skirts as it can get quite windy and most meals are eaten while sitting on the floor. 
  • Wear a hat or scarf to keep your head cool. 
  • Wear open-toed footwear like sandals or flip flops to avoid getting sand into your shoes.
