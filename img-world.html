Where is IMG Dubai located?

IMG Dubai is located right next to the Global Village, and is accessible by both car and public transport.

Address: E311, Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road, City of Arabia - Dubai - United Arab Emirates
Find on Google Map

How can I reach IMG Dubai by car?

The most convenient way to reach IMG Worlds of Adventure is by car. A taxi and private coach area can be found conveniently close to the entrance.

Can I reach IMG Dubai by metro?

Yes. Although IMG Dubai is not directly connected via the metro, you can get down at the Dubai Mall (Approx 25 km) or Mall of the Emirates (Approx 30 km) and hail a taxi.

How far is IMG Dubai from Abu Dhabi?

IMG Dubai is 120 km from Abu Dhabi and should not be more than 1 hour 15 minutes away by car.

What are IMG Worlds of Adventure's opening hours?

IMG is open every day of the week, throughout the year. Plan your visit to IMG with their updated timings:  
Sunday to Friday: 12:00 PM to 10:00 PM
Saturday: 12:00 PM to 11:00 PM
The Haunted Hotel is open from 3:00 PM to 9:00 PM.

What is the best time to visit IMG Dubai?

Since IMG is an indoor theme park, you can visit anytime of the year without worrying about Dubai's summer. However, it is one of the most-visited theme parks in Dubai. If you want to avoid the long queues before every ride at IMG Dubai, it's best to plan your visit on weekdays as you would generally find smaller crowds.

When is IMG closed?

IMG Dubai is open throughout the year.

What are my dining options at IMG Worlds of Adventure?

IMG Dubai has some amazing dining options in each adventure zone. You can snack on scrumptious shawarmas at Downtown Shawarma and indulge at smoothies at Mr Smoothy for the ultimate day out at this fun-filled theme park.

What are the facilities available at IMG Worlds?

  • Wheelchairs & strollers
  • Toilets

Can I shop at IMG Worlds?

For some themed souvenirs to remember your trip by, head to Marvel Universe or the Cartoon Network Store. You can also explore fun shopping options like World of Candy where you get to make your own candy or The Explorer's Supply, where you can stock up on "prehistoric relics."

Is IMG Worlds wheelchair accessible?

Yes, IMG Worlds is wheelchair accessible and hopes to provide a comfortable experience to its guests with disabilities. Here's a list of facilities IMG provides to make their experience a hassle-free one.

What are the rules I have to follow while visiting IMG Worlds?

Here's a list of IMG Worlds rules you can refer to while planning your visit:

  • Children aged 12 or under have to be accompanied by an individual aged 16 or older
  • Drinking and/or carrying alcohol within the premises of IMG Worlds is prohibited
  • Only tickets that bear the accurate date and time of visit are valid
  • Picnics are not allowed within the IMG Worlds premise
Can I carry food to IMG Worlds Dubai?

Since outside food is not allowed, you cannot carry your own food inside IMG. However, IMG houses a number of restaurants where you can dine in.

Frequently Asked Questions About Img Worlds of Adventure Dubai

Is it worth visiting IMG Worlds of Adventure?

Absolutely! It is the world's largest indoor theme park and offers an immersive environment through 5 themed zones for adults and children alike. Spend a great day choosing from 22 wonderful rides themed after Cartoon Network, Marvel, and more. It is one of the most-visited tourist destinations in Dubai for good reason!

Where can I book IMG Worlds of Adventure tickets?

You can choose to get your tickets either at the physical counter at IMG, or book them online through trusted partners like our's. However, waiting in long lines at the ticket counter could prove to be a waste of time, when you could be enjoying a hair-raising adventure. Simply book your IMG tickets online, skip-the-lines, and enjoy great discounts!

What are the different types of IMG tickets available?

You can book standard entry tickets which offer access to all rides and attractions at IMG, along with a meal voucher. Opt for fast-track tickets to skip the long lines at each ride, or go for combo tickets if you want access to not just IMG but other popular attractions in the city such as Burj Khalifa, Wild Wadi Waterpark, Dubai Frame, and more.

Is skip-the-line entry included in my IMG Worlds of Adventure tickets?

You would have to book specific fast track access tickets to get priority entry for each ride at IMG. These tickets would help you cut down your waiting time by up to 4 hours, allowing you to cover more rides and attractions at the park.

Is IMG Worlds of Adventure the world's largest indoor theme park?

Yes, IMG Worlds of Adventure is the world's largest indoor theme park. Spread across 4 zones, it houses 22 rides.

Which zones are present at IMG Worlds of Adventure Dubai?

Guests can go on rides across 4 zones at IMG: Cartoon Network, Marvel, IMG Boulevard, and Lost Valley.

When should I visit IMG Dubai?

From Sunday to Friday, visit between 12:00 PM to 10:00 PM and on Saturdays, visit between 12:00 PM to 11:00 PM. The best time to visit would be on weekdays, to avoid the weekend crowds. The best time of the year would be October, November, and January to April, as the summer months between May to September and winter month of December tend to get quite crowded due to the holiday season.

How can I get to IMG Worlds?

IMG Worlds of Adventure is located near Global Village. You can either take the taxi or the metro to IMG Worlds, although the latter would require that you get off at either the Dubai Mall station or the Mall of the Emirates station before getting a cab to the themepark. The easiest way to get to IMG Worlds is getting a taxi from where you're staying to the amusement park directly.

Is IMG Worlds suitable for young children?

Yes, there are plenty of rides and attractions that young children can enjoy at IMG Worlds.

Is wheelchair access available at IMG Worlds of Adventure?

Yes, several parts of the park have been designed so as to accommodate ease of movement for wheelchair users. Additionally, guests can also rent a wheelchair here.

Is storage facility available at IMG Dubai?

Yes. Guests can book lockers to store their belongings near the park entrance.

Small lockers: AED 45

Large: AED 55

Jumbo: AED 60

Is there a height restriction for rides at IMG Dubai?

Yes. If children visit unaccompanied, they should be at least 1.3 m or taller. If accompanied by adults or guardians, children must be 1.05 m or taller.
