The View at the Palm Tickets

The Palm Tower, the latest architectural marvel on Dubai's Palm Jumeirah, opened its doors to the public on 7 April 2021. An impressive 52-storied structure, the Palm Tower is a luxurious destination that is home to various commercial and residential spaces. However, the icing on this very tall cake is The View, a premier observation deck located on the 52nd floor, which offers unbeatable views of the Palm Jumeirah, Arabian Sea, and the city's impressive skyline. If you're looking for a new experience in Dubai that is sure to remain imprinted in your memory, book The View at the Palm tickets now.

Why Visit The View at the Palm?

The observation deck, located on the 52nd-floor of the Palm Tower, is called The View at the Palm. A worthwhile visit right to the top, this observation deck boasts a resplendent view of the architectural marvel that is the Palm Jumeirah. High above the ground, at about 240 m, The View at the Palm rivals most observation decks around the world with views of the sea, hundreds of trees, and towering skyscrapers.

The observation deck is connected to a luxurious lounge where you can relax and soak in the view as you indulge in a refreshing snack and drink. Adding to your experience is an interactive exhibit that talks about the Palm Tower in Dubai, and the city's past, present, and future.

What are the The View at the Palm's opening hours?

The View at the Palm is open every day of the week. From Sunday to Wednesday, it is open between 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM (final entry at 10:00 PM), and from Thursday to Saturday, between 10:00 AM to 12:00 AM (final entry at 11:00 PM).

What is the best time to visit The View at the Palm?

If you're looking for splendid sunset views, the best time to visit The View is during prime hours: 04:00 PM to 07:00 PM. If you're looking to avoid crowds and enjoy the panoramic scenery in peace, head up to The View on weekdays between 10:00 AM to 03:30 PM and 07:30 PM to closing.

Where is The View at the Palm located?

The observation deck is on the 52nd floor of the Palm Tower, which is located at Palm Jumeirah in Dubai.

How can I reach Palm Tower Dubai?

You can easily plan your visit to the Palm Tower in Dubai as it is accessible by metro, car, and taxi.

How do I reach Palm Tower Dubai by metro?

You can take the Palm Monorail that stops right in front of the tower. It is available throughout the day.

Things to Do at the Palm Tower Dubai

The View at the Palm

On the 52nd floor of this tower lies The View at the Palm observation deck. It's here that you will find mesmerizing views of the Palm Jumeirah, Arabian Sea, and Dubai's skyline.

The Falcon View

To further enhance your viewing experience, the Palm Tower will soon reveal a new observation deck – The Falcon View. This deck promises an immersive bird's eye, 360º view of the glittering city.

The Lounge

Relax and unwind with friends and family at the deluxe lounge on the 52nd floor. Like The View at the Palm, it allows you to soak in the beautiful view ahead, while you have a delicious meal or a refreshing drink.

St. Regis Hotel

The St. Regis Hotel at the Palm Tower oozes grandeur. A traveler favorite, this hotel boasts splendid, comfortable suites, award-winning dining, and various entertainment options.

Palm Jumeirah Attractions & Upcoming Projects

Along with the Palm Tower, the Palm Jumeirah is a one-stop destination for entertainment, shopping, and dining. Here are the top things to do at Palm Jumeirah.

Nakheel Mall

A new addition to Dubai's long list of elaborate malls, the Nakheel Mall promises to be a commercial space where all your needs will be met. It is home to over 300 international and local brands, a wide variety of cuisines, and a host of entertainment options.

The Pointe

A premier waterfront destination on the Palm Jumeirah, The Pointe, is a great place to spend a lovely evening. It is dotted with multiple retail stores and is home to restaurants catering to every visitor's cravings.

Atlantis, The Palm

The Atlantis hotel's reputation is simply stellar worldwide. Visitors from all around the world flock to the Atlantis, to stay at one of its lavish suites, visit the thrilling Aquaventure Waterpark, or explore the Lost Chambers aquarium.

Al Ittihad Park

If you're looking for a quiet spot to unwind, work, or go running, directly head to Al Ittihad Park. Covered in plants, trees, and more greenery, with a 3.2 km running track, it's the ideal place to spend some quality time with oneself.

Palm West Beach

Looking to laze by the sea? The Palm West Beach is ideal for some sun and sand fun with friends and family. With lovely little cafes and plenty of palm trees, this beach is not to be missed.

The Palm Fountain

Official dubbed the largest fountain in the world, The Palm Fountain puts on quite a spectacular performance! Prepare to be entertained by a resounding music-and-dance show, further enhanced by vibrant colors.

The View at the Palm Tickets FAQs

Is The View at the Palm open?

Yes, The View at the Palm opened to the public on 7 April 2021.

Are any COVID-19 safety measures in place?

Yes, tickets must be booked online. Masks and social distancing is mandatory at The View at the Palm. Temperature checks will be carried out at the entrance and the premises are thoroughly sanitized frequently.

Can I book The View at the Palm tickets online?

Yes. You can book your tickets online. This allows the venue to moderate visitor capacity, and also enables guests to choose their preferred date and time for a visit.

What do The View at the Palm tickets include?

The View at the Palm tickets offer direct entry into the Palm Tower and to the elevator that will take you straight up to the 52nd floor observation deck. Guests can comfortably spend an hour or more here.

What are View at the Palm ticket prices?

Tickets for visits during non-prime hours (10:00 AM to 03:30 PM and 07:30 PM to closing) are priced at AED 100, while tickets for prime hours (04:00 PM to 07:00 PM) are priced at AED 158.

Are any discounts available on The View at the Palm tickets?

Yes. Children under the age of 4 enjoy free entry, while those between ages 4 to 12 get reduced pricing on presentation of a valid ID.

What The View at the Palm opening hours?

The View at the Palm is open every day of the week. From Sunday to Wednesday, it is open between 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM (final entry at 10:00 PM), and from Thursday to Saturday, between 10:00 AM to 12:00 AM (final entry at 11:00 PM).

What is the best time to visit The View at the Palm?

Prime hours, between 04:00 PM to 07:00 PM offer spectacular sunset views and are definitely worth a visit. However, this is when you'll find larger crowds. If you're looking for smaller crowds, ideally visit during weekdays during non-prime hours, which is between 10:00 AM to 03:30 PM and 07:30 PM to closing.

How do I get to The View at the Palm?

The View at the Palm is an observation deck on the 52nd floor of the Palm Tower. The tower is located on Palm Jumeirah in Dubai. You can either drive down, take a taxi, or take the Palm Monorail to get there.
