All Your Questions About XLine Dubai Tickets Answered

Q. Where can I buy XLine Dubai tickets?

A. You can buy XLine Dubai tickets online for a hassle-free experience.

Q. Can I buy XLine Dubai tickets online?

A. Yes, you can book your XLine Dubai Marina tickets online.

Q. Why is XLine Dubai so famous?

A. The XLine Dubai is the world’s longest urban zipline that takes off from the top of Amwaj Towers at 170m in height and offers unparalleled views of the Dubai landscape.

Q. Is XLine Dubai open?

A. The XLine Dubai Marina is open all days of the week except for Tuesdays.

Q. What are the timings of the XLine Dubai Marina Zipline?

A. XLine Dubai Marina is open from Wednesday to Monday, except Tuesdays. You can choose your slot from the morning sessions from 10 am to 12:40 pm or the afternoon sessions from 1:40 pm to 5 pm. Slots are available every 20 min

Q. Who can ride XLine Dubai?

A. Any medically fit individual in the age range of 12 to 65 years weighing between 50-100 kgs and taller than 130 cm (4 ft 3 in) can take the ride. However, 12-18 year-olds need to be accompanied by a guardian on the premises.

Q. How fast will I go in the XLine Dubai Marina Zipline?

A. You can reach a maximum speed of 80 kmph. During your experience, you will get to accelerate from 0 to 80 kmph in a mere 2.5 seconds.

Q. How long is the XLine Dubai Marina Zipline?

A. The XLine Dubai Marina Zipline covers a distance of 1 km or approximately 0.6 miles.

Q. How high is the XLine Dubai platform?

A. The platform for take-off at XLine Dubai is 170 meters above the ground.

Q. How long does the ziplining experience last at XLine Dubai?

A. The whole process starting from registration at the XLine booth of the Dubai Marina Mall to the completion of the ride can take around 1 hour and 30 minutes.

Q. Is XLine Dubai worth trying?

A. The zipline Dubai experience is designed in a safe and secure manner to maximize the pure thrill and euphoria of flying for the average individual and adventure junkies alike.

Q. Is photography allowed in XLine Dubai Marina Zipline?

A. Personal cameras are not allowed during the zip lining experience. However, you will be strapped with a camera on your helmet to record your ride from the get-go.
