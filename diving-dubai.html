What is Dubai Deep Dive?

Looking for the perfect diving experience? Beginner or not, experienced or simply testing waters, Deep Dive Dubai is the perfect place for you! Ranging from experiences for inexperienced people looking to start diving, to expert divers, Deep Dive Dubai has a slew of options for everyone!

Deep Dive Dubai's pools were open to public from 21 July 2022, and boasts of highly advanced diving technologies , as well as a unique experience in the form of it's Sunken City - an underwaterscape created to enthrall those seeking to go diving into the deepest swimming pool in the world!

Know Before You Go

The LEGOLAND Water Park experience is attached to the main LEGOLAND  Dubai park and pulls in crowds of families from all over the world, looking to make fun memories across the rides, shows, and overall family experience.

  • Knowing swimming is a prerequisite for most experiences Deep Dive Dubai offers.
  • All necessary equipment is included in the ticket prices across all experiences. However, it is recommended you carry regular swimwear, a bathtowel and flipflops.
  • Make sure to check the terms and conditions of the experiences you book, especially if you intend to go with family as some experiences don't allow children under 10 to enter the waters.
  • All minors must be accompanied by an adult and should present written consent.

Deep Dive Dubai is one of the most popular tourist attractions in the city. It offers an exhilarating experience for both first-time and experienced divers. The company provides a comprehensive package that includes all the necessary equipment, training, and support needed to make your diving experience safe and enjoyable. Deep Dive Dubai also offer a variety of other services such as snorkeling, dolphin watching, and fish feeding.

The world's deepest pool at 60 meters, Deep Dive Dubai is guaranteed to offer you some of the most unique diving experiences. With safety as one of it's messaging pillars, Deep Dive Dubai offers suba and freediving instructions with the latest equipment.

There are many other experiences offered by this astonishing record breaking space that are worth exploring!

The entire experience has been crafted very thoughtfully, including well-placed cameras to record your experiences. There is also a highly developed sound system at Deep Dive to create underwater soundscapes, and can also be used to communute with other divers.

Inside Deep Dive Dubai

Deep Dive Dubai's extensive pools is not unique for just its size. The magnificent pool comes with an underwater cityscape to make for a dynamic experience for the divers, as opposed to the poolbed being made of merely by simple white tiles.

'The Sunken City' is fashioned after the concept of post-apocalyptic ruins of a city under water. The city can be explored at various depths by everyone taking part in the Deep Dive Dubai experiences, be it scuba diving, freediving or snorkelling. What makes this astonishing is that the cityscape underwater changes with each depth and gives the impression of nature taking over, the deeper you go!

  • At 0-25m: Storefronts, streetlights, ATMs, benches.
  • At 28m: A full apartment.
  • At 38m: A motor garage featuring cars, motorbikes, arcade games, etc.
  • At 40-60m: A deep shaft with brickwalls and graffiti.

A major attraction at Deep Dive Dubai, the Sunken City waterscapes can be accessed by everybody taking a dive into the pool! It has different aspects of a cityscape to be explored at different depths. All your scuba diving, freediving and snorkelling experiences at Deep Dive Dubai will allow you to explore different levels of the Sunken City!

The view is spectacular, and the restaurant would be a great way to end what would surely be a memorable, but exhausting experience.

Frequently Asked Questions About Deep Dive Dubai

What is Deep Dive Dubai?

Deep Dive Dubai is a one-of-a-kind diving destination that offers perfect conditions for diving all year round. In addition to being a great place to dive, Deep Dive Dubai also offers a range of courses for both beginner and experienced divers.

How do they keep Deep Dive Dubai clean?

The team at Deep Dive Dubai is committed to keeping the area clean and safe for all divers. They use a variety of methods to keep the water clean, including filtration, skimming, and circulation.

Who is the owner of Deep Dive Dubai?

The owner of Deep Dive Dubai is Fahad Al Rais. He is a certified diver and instructor who is passionate about making diving accessible to everyone.

What is the cost of membership at Deep Dive Dubai?

There is no membership fee to dive at Deep Dive Dubai. However, there are fees for some of the courses offered. For more information, please visit the website.

What is the minimum age requirement?

You need to be at least 10 years old to take part in the courses offered at Deep Dive Dubai.

Is Deep Dive Dubai man-made?

Yes it is. At a staggering 60 meters deep (nearly 200 feet), the pool has been recognized by Guinness World Records as the deepest dive pool ever created by man.

What should I bring to Deep Dive Dubai?

All you need to bring to Deep Dive Dubai is your bathing suit and towel. We will provide all the equipment you need for your dive.

How deep is Deep Dive Dubai?

With depths of up to 60 meters – 15 meters deeper and four times bigger than any other diving pool in the world – Deep Dive Dubai is one of the best underwater adventures offered!
