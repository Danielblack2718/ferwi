Visiting 3D World Dubai Trick-Art ‘Selfie’ Museum | Book 3D World Selfie Museum Dubai Tickets

Dubai boasts of an iconic 3D art museum with reality-defying hand-painted works. The unique museum is an interactive gallery where you can click pictures and selfies. The trick art here is inspired by a French traditional art technique called “Trompe-l’oeil” or “deceive the eye”. The 2-dimensional artwork on walls, floors and ceilings appears 3 dimensional.

Read on to find out more about the museum, the timings, highlights, attractions and tickets.

3D World Selfie Museum Dubai Highlights

Illusion

Here you’ll find hand-painted illusions that create an unbelievable effect. Use your imagination to strike the perfect pose. You can be amidst the fishes in the bowl or get trapped in a glass jar, the choice is yours.

Arabic

Fly the magic carpet or talk to the genie of the lamp! The Arab-themed paintings take you on a journey to the desert or let you travel back in time. Some even take you to the fabled lands of Arabian nights.

Egyptian

Travel to the pyramids of Giza and explore the ruins of ancient Egypt. You can pose as a mummy or explore the spooky alleyways of Egypt. Like the Arab zone, the Egyptian area lets you travel in time.

Water World

Strike a pose in front of ferocious sharks and crocodiles. You can also go on a surf or swim with the whales. The water world is a mix of fantasy and realistic animals. Where the art ends, your imagination begins. 

Animal Kingdom

In dense jungles, you’ll find dangerous tigers, elephants and other animals. Get ready to run away from dinosaurs or get your picture taken by a grizzly bear. The animal kingdom gives you the impression of a distant tropical land.

World Of Masterpieces

Remember Edvard Munch’s famous Scream? Here at the Dubai 3D museum, the scream has a hilarious twist. Standing amidst these paintings turned memes, don’t forget to appreciate the detail and its semblance to the original piece

Fantasy

The paintings here make your childhood stories come to life. Discover a magical new world in the Fantasy Zone. Whether it is the Magic carpet or the Genie in the lamp, they’re as real as paintings can get. From ancient folklore to modern pop culture, the fantasy zone has something for everyone.

Jungle

Take a walk amidst the tropical jungles. The visuals are so realistic that it makes you feel like you are walking through a jungle in Vietnam. Witness different plants and animals lurking between the trees.

Humor

Humor paintings are set in bizarre themes. You’ll find yourself striking funny poses to complement the paintings. Turn yourself into a meme and have a good laugh on your way back home. This zone is sure to tickle your funny bone.
