Dubai Miracle Garden, the World's Largest Natural Flower Garden, is Now Open!

Planning a trip to stunning Dubai? The city of gold is full to the brim with exciting landmarks and attractions. One such attraction that stands tall in Dubai's attractive tourist landscape is the Dubai Miracle Garden. Located in the Dubailand district in the heart of the city, the Dubai Miracle Garden is officially the world's largest natural flower garden. Given its popularity, Miracle Garden tickets sell like hotcakes and it's one of the most visited attractions in the city. What sets the Dubai Miracle Garden apart from other gardens and parks is the staggering fact that it houses more than 50 million flowers presented in unique and exciting ways to delight visitors. Read on to learn more about the attraction including things to see, Miracle Garden tickets, tips, and tricks for a memorable experience.

Why Visit Miracle Garden?

  • Constant Evolution: What makes the Miracle Garden Dubai truly miraculous is the fact that the decor and structure of the park change annually and follow a specific theme. Each time you visit the Miracle Garden, you'll see fresh new visuals making it a unique attraction.
  • Flowers & The Desert: There's something inherently interesting about fully bloomed flowers against the striking backdrop of Dubai's glorious desert. On a purely sensory level, the fragrance of bloomed flowers mingling with the balmy air of the desert is hard to replicate or experience anywhere else in the world.
  • Butterfly, Fly Away: A spectacular highlight of the Miracle Garden Dubai is the Dubai Butterfly Garden. Spread over 1800 square meters and featuring 9 domes that house butterflies in many varied colors, shapes, and sizes, the Dubai Butterfly Garden is officially the largest in the world.
  • Award-Winning Garden: The Miracle Garden Dubai isn't your typical garden, but an award-winning attraction that attracts millions of visitors annually. Apart from winning the Moselle Award, the Miracle Garden is also renowned for holding two Guinness Book of World Records for the tallest topiary structure and the largest floral installation.

Miracle Garden Dubai Highlights

With a place as vast and immersive as the Miracle Garden, it's a tall order to cover everything on offer. While there are numerous exhibitions and showcases in the Miracle Garden, we have hand-picked a few options you must-see during your trip.

Smurfs Village

Dubai's Miracle Garden has reopened with this all-new attraction that resembles the harmonious village of the small and blue creatures. The landscape includes a forest of mushroom-shaped houses, vibrant natural flowers and plants, Smurf topiaries or plant structures, and interesting activity areas.

Heart Tunnel

The perfect spot for a memorable picture, the Heart Tunnel features a dozen heart structures made exclusively from thousands of flowers. Created during the inception of the Miracle Garden Dubai, the Heart Tunnel has been a fixture ever since and functions as a passageway throughout the garden.

Airbus A380 & Trampoline Park

  • Airbus A380: Featuring beautiful marigolds, petunias, geraniums, and sunflowers, the Airbus A380 is a definite highlight of the Miracle Garden and faithfully recreates the iconic aircraft to every tiny detail. During the bloom season, the life-size replica features over 5 million flowers.
  • Trampoline Park: A relatively new addition to the Dubai Miracle Garden, the Trampoline Park is the perfect attraction for the whole family. Featuring 12 trampolines, the park doesn't just offer oodles of fun for young kids but is also equipped with all the required security features. The park is also equipped with trained in-house staff to ensure your children's safety during playtime.

Floral Castle & Lake Park

  • Floral Castle: The Floral Castle, like the name suggests, is a floral showcase in the shape of a massive castle. Featuring a comfortable seating and dining facility, the Floral Castle is a highlight of the Miracle Garden. Featuring glorious floral installations and beautiful landscaping, the Floral Castle contributes majorly to the beauty and appeal of the Miracle Garden.
  • Lake Park: One of the few water bodies in the Miracle Garden, Lake Park can be best described as a waterfront paradise. Boasting a stunning collection of flowers around an artificial lake, Lake Park is the perfect spot for a peaceful stroll after you're done exploring the rest of the Miracle Garden.

Dubai Butterfly Garden & Amphitheatre

  • Dubai Butterfly Garden: Located adjacent to Dubai Miracle Garden, the Dubai Butterfly Garden is one of the most popular attractions in the area. This butterfly sanctuary is home to approximately 15,000 butterflies from over 50 species. The recreational facility comprises 10 custom domes that are climate-controlled to allow the butterflies to be more comfortable and have plenty of space to fly around. 
  • Amphitheatre: Dubai Miracle Garden also has a huge floral Amphitheatre that was recently revamped. It serves as a stage for live entertainment, theatrical events, and celebrity visits.
