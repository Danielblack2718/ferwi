Games at Topgolf Dubai

TopGolf

Level(s): Beginner

A straightforward game, players earn points by hitting balls into a target. The more precise the shot and the longer the distance, the higher the points one gets.

TopScramble

Level(s): Intermediate

This requires two competing teams, of which only the highest scoring ball is added to the team score.

TopScore

Level(s): Beginner

Scores are multiplied based on the target hit:

  • Yellow: x2
  • Green: x4
  • Brown: x6
  • Blue: x8
  • White: x10

Quick 9

Level(s): Beginner & Advanced

Players get 3 shots each at the Red, Yellow, and Green (Beginner) or Brown, Blue, and White (Advanced) targets. The closer one hits to the center of each target, the higher the points they get. The last shot at each target is worth double the points.

TopPressure

Level(s): Beginner & Advanced

Players must hit all nine sections within a target (Yellow for Beginners, Red for Advanced). Finish the first level, and your points will be multiplied in levels 2 and 3.

For Advanced players: If you hit the same section twice, you lose points.

TopShot

Level(s): Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced

Players must hit five shots over four consecutive distances starting with Red (beginner), Yellow (intermediate), or Green (advanced) targets. Ball 15 is an extra ball and scoring inside every section apart from the outer ring gets you 10 additional points.

TopDrive

Level(s): Advanced

The game is based on the White target and The Trench. Should a player hit a ball outside of these areas, they get negative points as follows:

  • Red: -5
  • Yellow: -4
  • Green: -3
  • Brown: -2
  • Blue: -1

TopChip

Level(s): Intermediate

Players must hit targets at three consecutive distances starting with the Red target (5 in Red, 5 in Yellow, 10 in Green). Should they manage to score within the shaded area of the target, their next shot is worth double the points.

Visitor Rules

  • It is recommended you wear comfortable clothes and shoes to play in.
  • Outside foods and drinks are not permitted. The only exception to this rule is store-bought birthday cakes.
  • Players must hit from the mat, with no running starts.
  • All players should stand behind the red line at all times, except the one hitting the ball.
  • Players must check their surroundings before hitting a ball to avoid any injuries to the people around them.
  • Do not throw golf balls around.
  • Hitting golf balls over the boundary netting is not allowed, and players are responsible for any damages or injuries caused by any balls hit outside of the venue.
  • The venue is not responsible for any lost or stolen items.
  • Guests under 16 must be accompanied by a guest over the age of 21. After 9 PM, the same applies to guests under 18.
  • Guests aged 21 and above must carry a valid ID and show it when asked.
