Why Visit Dubai

With a population boom of over 500%, Dubai is one of the fastest-growing cities in the world! It is the global hub for trade and commerce and has grown to become a popular tourist destination, thanks to its state of the art infrastructure! The city takes immense pride in having the tallest and most innovative architecture worldwide, be it the Burj Khalifa or Dubai Frame, inviting thousands of visitors each year! 

What's more? The city not only preserves its rich heritage but it also embraces its multicultural diversity, capturing the true essence of UAE. No matter where you’re travelling from, the city can make you feel right at home. Discover the local souks to find gold jewellery, fabrics, perfumes and special souvenirs. Enjoy mouthwatering global cuisines including, meaty shawarmas, crispy burgers, cheesy pastas and more at food trucks or restaurants. 

The city highlights get taken to a new new level with Dubai’s attractions, bringing in the most crowd each year! Over the past 30 years, the city has transformed itself to become the ultimate hot spot for entertainment! Desert Safari, Dubai Dolphinarium, VR Park Dubai, Hatta Wadi Hub, Wild Wadi Waterpark and IMG World are few to be named! So what are you waiting for?   • Best Time for Beach Goers: March, April, Sept, Oct

  • Best Time for Shopaholics: Jan, July
  • Metro: Opt for a RED NOL Card for a single-use a day pass. Get a Silver/Gold/Personal card if you’re staying in Dubai for a longer duration.
  • Bus: The buses used by the RTA are air-conditioned, equipped with NOL card sensors at the entry & exit points and easily accessible by people with special needs.
  • Trams:  The tram line is currently limited and runs for 14.5 kilometres along Al Sufouh Road, connecting Dubai Marina to the Palm Jumeirah and Al Sufouh. You will need to get an NOL card to travel on the tram.
  • Monorail: The Dubai Monorail runs on the Palm Islands and connects it to the mainland with further plans to connect the line to the Metro Red Line. The Dubai Monorail accepts only cash.
  • Dubai Ferry: One of the newest forms of public transport in Dubai, the Dubai Ferry is also operated by the RTA. These modern air-conditioned sailing vessels operate on the Dubai Canal and the Dubai Marina.
  • Taxis: While taxis are expensive, it is one of the most convenient ways to move around the city.
  • Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo - Delve into the glorious Dubai Aquarium that houses several hundred marine species over 10 million litres
  • Dubai Frame - Get a stunning view of the Dubai Skyline at the Dubai Frame.
  • Burj Khalifa - Experience Dubai from up above the tallest building in the world at Burj Khalifa.
  • Dubai Dolphinarium - Enjoy a dolphin and seal extravaganza as these amazing creatures put their best fin forward with incredible acts like juggling, singing, and dancing. 
  • Laguna Waterpark - Discover one of the most exhilarating water parks in the world, as you encounter free falls and twists around the numerous water-themed rides in Laguna Waterpark.
  • IMG World - Explore over 4 adventure zones containing 22 rides, spread across 1.5 million square feet at the World’s Largest Indoor Theme Park - IMG World.

Where can I buy Dubai attraction tickets?

What can I do in Dubai on a budget?

There are lots of budget-friendly attractions in Dubai that are worth visiting. Dhow Cruise, Dubai Frame, Dubai Aquarium and VR Park are a few to be named.

Should I book my Dubai attraction tickets online?

Yes, it is highly recommended to book your Dubai attraction tickets online as you get the option to reserve a slot for the date and time of your choice.

Are there any discounts offered on any of the Dubai attraction tickets?

Yes, depending on the experience that you select, there are discounts offered on ticket prices for children at reduced rates.

Can I cancel my Dubai attraction tickets?

While many Dubai attraction tickets have a flexible cancellation policy, it can vary depending on which experience you book for.

What are the best attractions to see in Dubai?

There are so many popular attractions in Dubai that welcome thousands of visitors each year. Burj Khalifa, IMG World, Dubai Aquarium and Wild Wadi Waterpark are among the top must-see places in the city.

What are some free attractions that I can see in Dubai?

Visiting the Gold Souk, birdwatching at Ras Al Khor Wildlife Sanctuary, strolling through Zabeel Park, watching sunset at La Mer, hiking at Hatta are some of the free things to do in Dubai.
