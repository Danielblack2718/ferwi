What is Dubai LEGOLAND® Water Park?

At Dubai Legoland® Water Park, with multiple interactive experiences for children through themed rides, attractions, and shows, you are sure to have an exciting adventure splashing about the pools or zooming through the multiple slides. Dubai LEGOLAND Water Park is the only adventure waterpark designed for kids between 2-12 years in the Middle East. The theme park offers a world of fun all year round. Read on to find out how you can book different LEGOLAND water park tickets and other highlights about the experience. 

Highlights Of LEGOLAND® Water Park in Dubai

The LEGOLAND Water Park experience is attached to the main LEGOLAND  Dubai park and pulls in crowds of families from all over the world, looking to make fun memories across the rides, shows, and overall family experience.

  • Themed adventure: Bring your kid’s LEGO® toy dreams to life at the park as they get to play in a life-sized LEGO-themed playground and interact with many popular characters in the experience.
  • Child-centered: The LEGOLAND water park is the only water park in the UAE centered around children of a younger age group between 2-12, making it entirely focused on kids, providing a safe experience with wholesome adventures for your children.
  • Build-your-own-adventure: LEGOLAND offers an entirely unique and creative experience for all visitors, with experiences like Build-A-Boat, and Build-A-Raft. You also get to splash through the waves with their creations for a safe and fun adventure.
  • Family zones: Enjoy with the whole family, including your toddlers, in the family rides, including the Joker Soaker – an interactive water playground, the LEGO® wave pool, the Splash Safari – for toddler-sized slides, and the Build with Lego experiences.
  • Adrenaline pumping slides: Slightly older children can take on the multiple exhilarating slide rides, including the Wave Rider, Tidal Tube, Splash Out, and other faster rides.

Things To Do at LEGOLAND® Water Park

There are loads of things for you to enjoy during your visit to LEGOLAND Waterpark. Here’s a list of experiences you can enjoy at the park.

Rides

Explore the 13 rides of the waterpark from smaller zones like the Joker Soaker, and Splash Safari, for toddlers and kids; to bigger rides like the Red Rush, Splash Out, Wave Riders, etc. Take a short break in the LEGO® wave pool, Build-A-Boat for family fun, Build-A-Raft for the lazy river, and chill in the sun.

Visitor Tips & Guidelines

Check out these tips and important things to know before visiting the LEGOLAND Water Park for a fun, family experience:

  • Food and Beverages: While outside food is not allowed in the park, you can pack baby food and meals required for certain dietary accommodations, as they will be allowed inside. 
  • Height requirements: Please check out the height requirements for different rides before you visit so you can plan accordingly and avoid disappointment.
  • Arrive early: Dodge the crowds at the park by arriving during the early opening hours.
  • Rent a locker: It is easy to lose track of things at a waterpark, and thus it’s best to rent a storage locker to avoid lugging multiple things around. 
  • Preparation: Wear comfortable clothes and carry essentials, including water, sunscreen, and other items. 

Frequently Asked Questions About LEGOLAND® Water Park Theme Park

Q. What is LEGOLAND® Water Park?

A. LEGOLAND Water Park is the only waterpark in the Middle East built for children between the ages of 2-12. Built in the LEGO® theme and attached to the main park, the waterpark is a wholesome adventure for the whole family.

Q. Where can I buy LEGOLAND® Water Park tickets?

A. You can buy LEGOLAND Water Park tickets online.

Q. Where is LEGOLAND® Water Park located?

A. The LEGOLAND Water Park is located at Dubai Parks and Resorts in Jebel Ali South, off of Sheikh Zayed Road, 20 minutes away from The Dubai Marina.

Q. What are the LEGOLAND® Water Park opening hours?

A. The LEGOLAND Water Park is open from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. every day, except on Wednesdays.

Q. What’s the best time to visit LEGOLAND® Water Park?

A. The best time to visit is early opening hours as there are smaller crowds then.

Q. How much time do you need at LEGOLAND® Water Park?

A. You can easily spend nearly 4 hours at the LEGOLAND Water Park.

Q. Are there lockers and storage facilities available at LEGOLAND® Water Park?

A. Yes, lockers are available to be rented, next to the changing rooms in LEGOLAND Water Park.

Q. Can I leave my children alone at LEGOLAND® Water Park?

A. No, you are expected to accompany your children below the age of 13 at the park at all times.

Q. Is there a parking space inside LEGOLAND® Water Park?

A. There is a dedicated parking space for the waterpark, but non-pass holders and special ticket holders will have to pay for parking.

Q. Are food and beverages allowed into LEGOLAND® Water Park?

A. Outside food and beverages are not permitted and are subject to confiscation on entry, except for baby foods and dietary accommodations.

Q. What type of restaurants does LEGOLAND® Water Park offer?

A. LEGOLAND Water Park has two dining locations offering fresh meals, including salads, sandwiches, and wraps.

Q. Are strollers and wheelchairs available inside LEGOLAND® Water Park?

A. Yes, they are available upon request, on entry, in LEGOLAND Water Park.

Q. Is the LEGOLAND® Water Park disability friendly?

A. Most attractions at the park are accessible to our disabled guests, depending on the nature of the disability.

Q. Can I get First Aid at LEGOLAND® Water Park?

A. You can get First Aid at LEGOLAND® Water Park, next to the Baby Care Center and Waves Bistro.

Q. Is there a baby changing room inside LEGOLAND® Water Park?

A. Yes, the LEGOLAND® Water Park has a Baby Care Center, which functions as a private area for nursing, changing, infant play area, and preparing baby food.

Q. Is it worth visiting LEGOLAND® Water Park?

A. The LEGOLAND® Water Park is a themed waterpark experience for your kids and the kid in you. Enjoy fun rides for every age, and build memories for a lifetime.
